About GeekRoom

Geek Room is a widespread coding community with over 7500+ active coders nationwide. It was started by three 1st year coding enthusiasts with the main objective to create a transparent community where sharing of ideas and helping other people is the main goal . Geek Room boasts of various hackathon winning teams , 6 independent institutes with Geek Room chapters and so much more.

Our vast connections, a widespread community of over 7500+ coders nationwide, conduction of successful hackathons and events , boasting multi hackathon winning teams comprised purely of Geek Room members , a philosophy of a community of the coders , for the coders and by the coders and a transparent community where anyone can start coding easily , Geek Room is a unique trendsetter that is built for the upliftment of the Indian coding community.

Geekathon'24 FAQ's

What is Geekathon 2024?

Geekathon 2024 is a hybrid hackathon organized by GeekRoom KRMU in collaboration with KRMU's School of Engineering and Technology and Unstop. It's a platform for tech enthusiasts to build innovative solutions, collaborate with peers, and compete for exciting prizes.

Who can participate in Geekathon 2024?

Geekathon 2024 is open to all coding enthusiasts, from K.R. Mangalam University. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced coder, you're welcome to join the hackathon and showcase your skills. Form a team, brainstorm ideas, and get ready to code!

How can I register for Geekathon 2024?

To register for Geekathon 2024, click on the "Register Now" button on the homepage and follow the instructions. Be sure to complete your registration before the deadline to secure your spot.

What are the prizes for Geekathon 2024?

Geekathon 2024 offers exciting prizes, including 30,000 INR prize pool, certificates of participation, and exclusive merchandise. So, bring your A-game, showcase your skills, and compete for the ultimate geek title!

How can I get more information about Geekathon 2024?

If you have any questions or need more information, contact the GeekRoom team via email or social media. You can also join the GeekRoom community to stay updated on the latest hackathons, events, and coding resources.
